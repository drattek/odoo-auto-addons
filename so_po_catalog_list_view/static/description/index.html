Simplify your Sales and Purchases orders with Catalog List View module !

Description

Enhance your Odoo experience with our Catalog View List module, designed to streamline the management of purchase and sales orders. This module provides a comprehensive catalog view that allows you to easily browse, add, or remove products directly from the catalog list. Any changes made are instantly reflected in your purchase or sales orders, ensuring real-time updates and a seamless workflow. Simplify your order management process and gain full control over your product listings with this essential tool.

Features

  • Comprehensive Catalog View: Comprehensive Catalog View: A detailed, user-friendly catalog that displays all products related to purchase and sales orders.
  • Direct Product Management: Add, remove, or modify products directly from the catalog view without navigating away from the page.
  • Real-Time Synchronization: Instant updates to purchase and sales orders as changes are made in the catalog, ensuring your data remains accurate and current.
  • Seamless Workflow Integration: Enhances the order management process by integrating directly with Odoo's existing order management systems.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Simplifies the management of product listings with an intuitive and easy-to-navigate interface.
  • Efficient Order Handling: Streamlines the process of managing orders by consolidating product management into a single view.
  • Customizable Views: Adjust the catalog view to fit your specific needs and preferences for better control over your product listings.
  • Improved Productivity: Reduces the time spent managing products and orders, allowing for more efficient use of resources.
